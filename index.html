Our Story

From Adversity to Purpose

Chase Rice's personal motto, "Head Down, Eyes Up... Keep Going," was born during his junior year at the University of North Carolina. After being injured during the opening game of a football season where he was poised to become a breakout star, Chase faced both physical rehabilitation and emotional challenges.

While rehabbing his ankle injury, he caught his reflection in the mirror in a way he never had before: his head was down, but his eyes were up‚Äîready to do everything he could to fight for success and redemption. This moment of clarity transformed his perspective and eventually inspired the creation of the Chase Rice Foundation.

Today, this philosophy guides our mission to inspire people to "Keep Going" through extraordinarily difficult circumstances by keeping their head down and fighting every step of the way while focusing their vision on each individual challenge ahead.
