The Chase Rice Foundation is dedicated to empowering young musicians and supporting vulnerable communities through the transformative power of music education.

Our Story

From Adversity to Purpose

Chase Rice's personal motto, "Head Down, Eyes Up... Keep Going," was born during his junior year at the University of North Carolina. After an injury sidelined his football career, Chase faced a pivotal moment of reflection.

While rehabbing, he caught his reflection: his head was down, but his eyes were up—ready to fight for redemption. This philosophy now guides our mission to inspire people to "Keep Going" through life's most difficult circumstances.

Today, we focus that same determination on empowering the next generation through music, providing the tools and vision needed to overcome their own challenges.

Building Music Stars

Our flagship initiative provides musical instruments and education to young people in underprivileged communities.

Instruments Provided

We provide guitars, drums, pianos, and more to schools in need, ensuring every child has access to quality instruments.

Young Musicians

We empower young stars to build their interest in music and develop productive skills that foster lifelong growth.

Community Impact

Focusing on vulnerable children in underprivileged places, especially those with limited access to outside support.

Why Music Education Matters

Music education doesn't just improve musical skills—it provides productive activity that helps young people stay engaged and away from harmful influences. Your donation directly supports providing instruments to schools where young stars can build their talents.
